    The units in which the values in a value domain are measured. Adding unit of measure information makes the value more meaningful, especially in instances where a concept can be measured using various units of measure. For example, distance may be measured in kilometres or miles, while length may be measured in millimetres, centimetres, inches, metres, feet etc.

    While units of measure are specifically related to value domains, they are impertive to the formation of successful data elements.
